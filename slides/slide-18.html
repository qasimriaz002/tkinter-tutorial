Pack: Expand & Propagate

expand=True

Tells the parent container to allocate any extra space to this widget's slot.

pack_propagate(False)

By default, a Frame shrinks to fit its children. pack_propagate(False) forces the Frame to keep its specified size, regardless of what is inside it. This is critical for fixed-size backgrounds.

Button 1 (Expand)
Button 2

Button 1 fills the extra space.

import tkinter as tk root = tk.Tk() frame = tk.Frame(root, width=400, height=300, bg="yellow") frame.pack_propagate(False) frame.pack() btn1 = tk.Button(frame, text="I Expand", bg="lightblue") btn1.pack(expand=True, fill="both") btn2 = tk.Button(frame, text="I am small") btn2.pack() root.mainloop()
