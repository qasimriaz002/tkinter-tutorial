Geometry: Grid()

Grid divides the parent into rows and columns. It is the most powerful manager for form-like layouts.

Weights:

By default, columns don't resize. To make a column expand, you must configure its weight.

root.columnconfigure(1, weight=1) root.rowconfigure(1, weight=1)
